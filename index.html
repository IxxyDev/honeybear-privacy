Privacy Policy

Honeybear — Subscription Tracker · Last updated: March 10, 2026

Overview

Honeybear is designed with privacy at its core. The app runs entirely on your device and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All subscription data you enter (names, prices, billing dates, notes) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it (e.g., CSV export).

No Tracking

Honeybear does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. There are no third-party SDKs that collect user data.

Network Requests

The app makes a single type of network request:

  • Currency exchange rates — The app fetches publicly available exchange rate data from FloatRates (floatrates.com) to convert subscription prices between currencies. No personal information is sent with these requests.

In-App Purchases

Honeybear uses Apple's StoreKit framework for optional in-app purchases (Pro upgrade and tips). All transactions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, the app schedules local notifications on your device. These notifications are processed entirely by iOS and do not involve any external server.

UserDefaults

The app uses UserDefaults to store your preferences (theme, base currency, notification settings, onboarding status). This data stays on your device.

Children's Privacy

Honeybear does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
