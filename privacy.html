Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 8, 2026

Honeybear (“the app”) is a subscription tracker for iOS. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. The short version: your data stays on your device, and we do not collect, sell, or share personal information.

Data You Enter

Subscriptions you add — names, prices, billing cycles, dates, colors, and reminder settings — are stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to us or to any third party. If you have iCloud enabled for the app, your data may sync across your own devices through your private iCloud account, governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

Network Activity

The app makes a single type of network request: fetching public currency exchange rates from a third-party rates API. These requests contain no personal data and are not tied to your identity. Rates are cached on your device.

Notifications

Renewal reminders are scheduled locally on your device through the system notification framework. No notification data leaves your device.

Purchases

In-app purchases and subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. The app never sees or stores your payment details. Purchase and restore status is verified through Apple’s StoreKit. See Apple’s Privacy Policy for how Apple handles payment data.

Analytics & Tracking

The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. We do not use cookies or device identifiers for tracking.

Children

The app is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
