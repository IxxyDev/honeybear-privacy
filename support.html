Frequently Asked

Is the app free?
Yes. Honeybear is completely free and every feature is unlocked. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

Where is my data stored?
All your subscription data is stored locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Why don’t exchange rates update?
Rates are fetched from a public API and cached. If you’re offline, the app uses the last known rates.
